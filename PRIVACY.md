Privacy Policy for WhisperShroom

Last updated: March 23, 2026

WhisperShroom is a voice-to-text desktop application. This policy explains what data the app handles.


Data Collection and Processing

Audio Data: WhisperShroom records audio from your microphone only while you actively hold the configured hotkey. Audio recordings are sent directly to the OpenAI Whisper API for transcription and are not stored locally or by the developer.

API Key: Your OpenAI API key is stored locally on your device in a configuration file (%APPDATA%\WhisperShroom\config.json). It is never transmitted to any server other than the OpenAI API.

Transcription Results: Transcribed text is displayed in the app and copied to your clipboard. It is not stored persistently or sent anywhere beyond your device.

Settings: Your app preferences (microphone selection, hotkey, language) are stored locally on your device.


Third-Party Services

This app uses the OpenAI Whisper API to perform speech-to-text transcription. Audio data sent to OpenAI is subject to OpenAI's privacy policy (https://openai.com/privacy). No other third-party services are used.


Data We Do NOT Collect

- No analytics or telemetry
- No user accounts or registration
- No personal information beyond what is described above
- No data is sent to the developer


Data Storage

All data (API key, settings) is stored locally on your device. The developer has no access to any of your data.


Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at https://github.com/shroomlife/whisper-shroom/issues
